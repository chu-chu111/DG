Executive Profile

As the Director General of the Raw Materials Research and Development Council (RMRDC), Dr. Nnayelugo Martin Ike-Muonso provides strategic leadership in advancing Nigeria's raw materials sector through research, innovation, and sustainable development initiatives.

Under his leadership, RMRDC continues to play a pivotal role in identifying, developing, and promoting the utilization of Nigeria's vast raw material resources, fostering industrial growth, and supporting the nation's economic diversification agenda.

Leadership Focus Areas

  • Strategic development of Nigeria's raw materials sector
  • Research and innovation in materials science
  • Industrial technology transfer and commercialization
  • Sustainable resource utilization and management
  • Public-private sector collaboration and partnerships
  • Capacity building and human capital development

About RMRDC

The Raw Materials Research and Development Council was established to develop and coordinate policies on the sourcing, exploitation, and utilization of Nigeria's abundant raw material resources. The Council serves as the apex organization for raw materials research and development in Nigeria.
