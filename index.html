Executive Profile

As the Director General of the Raw Materials Research and Development Council (RMRDC), Dr. Nnayelugo Martin Ike-Muonso provides strategic leadership in advancing Nigeria's raw materials sector through research, innovation, and sustainable development initiatives.

Under his leadership, RMRDC continues to play a pivotal role in identifying, developing, and promoting the utilization of Nigeria's vast raw material resources, fostering industrial growth, and supporting the nation's economic diversification agenda.

Leadership Focus Areas

  • Strategic development of Nigeria's raw materials sector
  • Research and innovation in materials science
  • Industrial technology transfer and commercialization
  • Sustainable resource utilization and management
  • Public-private sector collaboration and partnerships
  • Capacity building and human capital development

About RMRDC

The Raw Materials Research and Development Council was established to develop and coordinate policies on the sourcing, exploitation, and utilization of Nigeria's abundant raw material resources. The Council serves as the apex organization for raw materials research and development in Nigeria.

Core Mandate

Our Primary Objectives and Responsibilities

Research & Development

Conducting cutting-edge research on raw materials and their applications to drive innovation and industrial growth.

Resource Mapping

Identifying and cataloguing Nigeria's raw material deposits to maximize utilization opportunities.

Industrialization

Promoting local utilization and value addition to raw materials for economic development.

Collaboration

Fostering partnerships between research institutions and industries for knowledge transfer.

Strategic Priorities

Our Focus Areas for National Development

Innovation & Technology

Advancing research in materials science, biotechnology, and sustainable processing technologies to maximize the value of Nigeria's raw materials.

Economic Development

Supporting Nigeria's economic diversification through the development of raw material-based industries and value chains.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible extraction and utilization of raw materials for long-term sustainability.

Capacity Building

Developing human capital and institutional capacity for effective raw materials research and industrial development.
